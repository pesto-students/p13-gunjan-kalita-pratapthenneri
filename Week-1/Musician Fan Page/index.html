M.M. Keeravani

MM Keeravani, also known as M. M. Kreem, is a renowned Indian film composer and playback singer. He was born on July 4, 1961, in Kovvur, Andhra Pradesh, India. Keeravani is popular for his exceptional work in various languages, including Telugu, Tamil, Hindi, Kannada, and Malayalam films. He has composed music for several blockbuster movies and is known for his ability to create soulful and mesmerizing melodies. His compositions have won numerous awards and accolades, making him one of the most respected and influential music directors in the Indian film industry.

Recently he got Oscar Award for the Best Original Song Category for the movie RRR.
