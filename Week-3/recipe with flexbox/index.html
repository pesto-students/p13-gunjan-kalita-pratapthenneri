OATMEAL COCONUT PANTRY COOKIES Chef Roman, August 22, 2023

Prep time: 30 minutes

Cooking time: 9-13 minutes

Skill level: Easy

Makes: 24 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Butter
  • 2 ½ cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • ½ cup Toasted Coconut

Directions

  1. Toast Coconut at 35° F for about 10-15 minutes.
  2. Beat Sugar, Butter and eggs
  3. Bake at 375° F for about 9-13 minutes.
